What is AppleToken?

Striving to reward its community, AppleToken has three main features.
  • A) Static Rewards: balance of all HOLDRs increases automatically.
  • B) Automatic Liquidity: a percentage of every transaction gets deposited into the liquidity pool.
  • C) Burning: AppleToken's are burned at notable market events raising the token price and mitigating market influence on the token.

Tokenomics

Two fees apply to every transaction.
  • A) 5% for automatic liquidity,
  • B) 5% for static rewards.
This way, every transaction benefits HOLDRs while safeguarding the future of the community. The distribution plan is as follows: 40% for presale, 25% for liquidity, 20% for burning, 8% for exchanges and, 7% for the AppleToken team. Team members will spend a large portion of their funds improving the project for the community.

Liquidity

Of the 5% liquidity fee, one-half gets converted into BNB. The resulting BNB-AppleToken pair gets deposited into the liquidity pool on PancakeSwap and is locked there forever. This feature provides benefits such as reduced price volatility and increased liquidity available to traders. Ultimately, automatic liquidity protects the community for years to come.

Rewards

Remember the 5% static rewards fee? That gets charged at every transaction. From there, it is redistributed back to the HOLDRs in proportion to how much AppleToken they already have. HOLDR rewards encourage buying and discourage selling which facilitates a higher price.

Charity

Everyone hates fraudulent charities. Not to worry, AppleToken features transparent charity donations. The community may vote for charities of their liking and fund them as they please. The charity wallets are made public, and it's all stored on the blockchain where everyone can see the funds.

Manual Burning

The AppleToken team will conduct manual burns at select real-life market events. Price drops caused by such events are either reduced or prevented entirely as a result. The team has reserved 20% of the total supply for burning alone.
